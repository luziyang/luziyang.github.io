SpringMVC(常用XML文件配置)

发表于 2016-10-13

1、项目启动时过滤Servlet请求（配置在web.xml文件中）

  <servlet>
    <servlet-name>luziyangSpring</servlet-name>
    <servlet-class>org.springframework.web.servlet.DispatcherServlet</servlet-class>
    <init-param>
         <param-name>contextConfigLocation</param-name>
         <param-value>/WEB-INF/root-context.xml</param-value>
    </init-param>
    <load-on-startup>1</load-on-startup>
  </servlet>

<servlet-mapping>
<servlet-name>luziyangSpring</servlet-name>
<url-pattern>/</url-pattern>
</servlet-mapping>
解释：将所有的Controller请求过滤进来，转入root-context.xml文件
2、中文处理（web.xml文件中）

<filter>
        <filter-name>characterEncodingFilter</filter-name>
        <filter-class>org.springframework.web.filter.CharacterEncodingFilter</filter-class>
        <init-param>
            <param-name>encoding</param-name>
            <param-value>UTF-8</param-value>
        </init-param>
        <init-param>
            <param-name>forceEncoding</param-name>
            <param-value>true</param-value>
        </init-param>
    </filter>
    <filter-mapping>
        <filter-name>characterEncodingFilter</filter-name>
        <url-pattern>/*</url-pattern>
    </filter-mapping>

3、视图解析器（配置在root-context.xml文件中）

 <!– 视图解析器 –>
    <bean class=”org.springframework.web.servlet.view.InternalResourceViewResolver”>
        <property name=”suffix” value=”.jsp”/> <!– 视图后缀,controller中的方法返回的url字符串会添加该后缀 –>
        <property name=”prefix” value=”/WEB-INF/jsp/“/> <!– 视图后缀controller中的方法返回的url字符串会添加该前缀 –>
    </bean>

4、时间格式转化（配置在root-context.xml文件中）

<?xml version=”1.0” encoding=”UTF-8”?>
<beans xmlns=”http://www.springframework.org/schema/beans“
    xmlns:xsi=”http://www.w3.org/2001/XMLSchema-instance“
    xmlns:mvc=”http://www.springframework.org/schema/mvc“
    xmlns:p=”http://www.springframework.org/schema/p“
    xmlns:context=”http://www.springframework.org/schema/context“
    xsi:schemaLocation=”
        http://www.springframework.org/schema/beans
        http://www.springframework.org/schema/beans/spring-beans.xsd
        http://www.springframework.org/schema/context
        http://www.springframework.org/schema/context/spring-context.xsd
        http://www.springframework.org/schema/mvc
        http://www.springframework.org/schema/mvc/spring-mvc.xsd"&gt;

&lt;context:component-scan base-package="com.luziyang"/&gt;
&lt;mvc:default-servlet-handler/&gt;
 &lt;!-- 启动转换器 --&gt;
&lt;mvc:annotation-driven conversion-service="conversionService"/&gt;
&lt;!-- 注册转换器 --&gt;
&lt;bean id="conversionService"
    class="org.springframework.format.support.FormattingConversionServiceFactoryBean"&gt;
&lt;/bean&gt;

</beans>
配置完在实体类的日期属性前加上注释@DateTimeFormat(pattern = “yyyy-MM-dd hh:mm:ss”)
